Step 2 — Basic RAG (AI Knowledge Search)
🎯 Goal

Build an AI service that can:

Upload company documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
Read and understand them
Store them in a vector database
Answer user questions using those documents

At the end of Step 2, you should be able to ask:

"What is the leave policy?"

and receive the correct answer from the uploaded document.

6. Embeddings

A sentence

becomes
